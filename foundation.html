FOUNDATION

Foundation is a liquid or powder makeup applied to the face to create an even, uniform color to the complexion, cover flaws and, sometimes, to change the natural skin tone. Some foundations also function as a moisturizer, sunscreen, astringent or base layer for more complex cosmetics.


Here's the annoying thing about foundation: When it's expertly applied, it can turn your face into a perfectly even, bright, smooth-looking pearl. But when it’s applied, uh, not so well, it has the potential to highlight fine lines you didn’t know existed, make your skin look flaky and oily, and leave you with a chalky-looking mask. Fun! Unfortunately for me, but luckily for you, I have experienced both the bad and the good sides of foundation. Which means I've now got a lifetime of tips and tricks that will revolutionize your makeup routine, so you'll never deal with another patchy, shiny, heavy mess again.

The struggle is real! Choosing the right foundation for your specific skin type and ensuring it’s the right shade is a strenuous task. It’s tricky with the multiple texture and shade options.

The way to know that you have picked the right shade of foundation, is when you look in the mirror and you can’t tell that there’s any product on your face. Just brighter, flawless and glowing skin. Always double check the shade in natural sunlight to see its effect under strong light. Finally, remember that there’s a high chance that our skin gets darker in the summer, so be ready to tweak the shade as the seasons change.Oily skin needs an oil-free powder or mousse foundation. The powders in them work toward absorbing the excess oil from your skin to offer a luxuriously smooth and matte look. The Lakme Absolute Mattreal Skin Natural Mousse is designed to offer a feather-light finish with a clean matte look

Now you’re good to go! Cleanse, tone, and moisturize. Apply your primer. Once it’s settled, open up your foundation and let the magic begin. Gorgeous skin in a jiffy!
